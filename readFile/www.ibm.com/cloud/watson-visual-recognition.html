What is Watson Visual Recognition?

The IBM Watson™ Visual Recognition service uses deep learning algorithms to analyze images for scenes, objects, faces, and other content.

Watson Visual Recognition features

Classify virtually any visual content

Visual Recognition understands the contents of images. Analyze images for scenes, objects, faces, colors, food, and other subjects that can give you insights into your visual content.

Create your own classifiers

Create and train your custom image classifiers using your own image collections.

Broad industry use cases

Use cases include manufacturing, visual auditing, insurance, social listening, social commerce, retail and education.
