What is Watson Knowledge Catalog?

IBM Watson Knowledge Catalog powers intelligent, self-service discovery of data, models and more, activating them for artificial intelligence, machine learning and deep learning. Access, curate, categorize and share data, knowledge assets and their relationships, wherever they reside.

Fuel AI with data

Curate and shape analytic assets, including machine learning models and structured and unstructured data, using Watson Natural Language Understanding, to fuel data science and all forms of AI, including ML and DL.

Modern cataloging

Intelligent recommendations on the most relevant assets powered by Watson, and discover more relevant assets quicker than ever before.

Trusted data & AI

Automatic, dynamic masking of sensitive data elements unlocks information previously unavailable to AI application development and powers collaborative development, making data a trusted enterprise asset while remaining protected from misuse.

Easier data preparation

Interactively discover, cleanse, and transform your data using Data Refinery. Understand the quality and distribution of your data with built-in charts and statistics.
