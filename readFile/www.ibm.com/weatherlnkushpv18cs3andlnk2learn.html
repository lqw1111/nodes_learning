Make better decisions with weather data

The Weather Company, an IBM Business, offers the most accurate weather forecasts globally with personalized and actionable insights to millions of consumers and thousands of businesses. Our solutions connect newscasters, airline pilots, energy traders, insurance executives, state agency employees, retail management and more to the weather intel they need, on any device. We aggregate the deepest, richest data sets — both business and consumer — to deliver personal, reliable and actionable weather information, analytics and insight.
