With Watson Growth

For anyone exploring or starting to use Watson Services.

Key Benefits
— Introduction to a community of people starting their AI journey
— Access to a variety of tech and marketing content

Requirements
— Access to this tier is open to anyone interested or starting to build using Watson services

With Watson Premier

For organizations with applications built with Watson Services that are looking to accelerate their AI transformation and business benefits

Key Benefits
— Application verification: Get your application verified by IBM for technical viability and usability.
— Expert business and technical help: Access courses and invite-only small group sessions to help you commercialize and refine your use case.
— Brand Access: Use of the With Watson badge within your App and marketing collateral
— Technical support: Assigned Customer Success manager and up to 8 hours of technical support per month
— Marketing support: Access to IBM marketers as well as the potential for co-marketing, case studies, speaking engagements and workshops
— Watson Beta Access: Receive early access to new services and product updates from the Watson product team.

Requirements
— Leverages Watson technology (e.g. Watson Services, Watson Knowledge Studio or Watson offerings) with consistent monthly usage and $50,000 minimum in annual spend
— App must be in market and perform as expected with no obvious bugs, errors, or instability
— App is free of any material that could be considered illegal, offensive, derogatory, mean-spirited, or in poor taste
— With Watson branding must be approved and used in accordance with the With Watson Brand Guidelines

With Watson Strategic

For organizations pursuing a large-scale projects with IBM Watson that requires joint investment and commitment.

Key Benefits
Strategic partners received dedicated support from IBM. Core benefits of a strategic partnership may include:
— Marketing: Custom marketing and co-marketing plan developed alongside the IBM Watson Marketing team
— Technology: Unlimited technical support and resources dedicated to the strategic partnership
— Branding: Exclusive With Watson and IBM Watson branding elements only available for strategic partners

Requirements
Strategic partnerships require a significant financial and technical commitment and are evaluated on a case by case basis.
