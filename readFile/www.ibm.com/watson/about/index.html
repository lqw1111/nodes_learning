Powered by the latest innovations in machine learning, Watson lets you learn more with less data. Build with your domain expertise

AI systems are only as smart as the people who train them.

From healthcare and education to finance, transportation, and energy, Watson is trained by leading experts in your field. It understands the language of your industry and taps into deep domain knowledge to help you make more informed decisions faster.

AI built for data

Watson can ingest, enrich and normalize a wide variety of data types without any additional integration, allowing you to leverage data from a broad range of sources with ease.

It also offers persistent data stores, robust data science tools, and a multitude of other features to help accelerate your company’s shift to becoming a data-driven organization.
