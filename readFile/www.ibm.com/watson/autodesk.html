Speeding Customer Service by 99% with Watson

Autodesk has found an innovation and efficient way for handling customer inquiries.Recognizing that their shiftto a subscription business model requires real-time customer service and support, Autodesk has employed AVA (Autodesk Virtual Agent), powered by Watson Conversation,to resolve customer issues more quickly and around the clock, giving Autodesk the cognitive competitive advantage.

The Challenge: Taking Risks by Changing Business Models

Because of Autodesk’s heavy volume of customer queries(roughly one million customer and partner contacts per year)-resolution time for inquiries was 1.5 days or longer. And, with their move to subscriptionbusinessmodel, they needed to scale for even more questions.
